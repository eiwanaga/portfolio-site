Annual Report

OBJECTIVE

Design the cover of the Kapi'olani Community College annual report.

RESULTS

The Annual Report covers events, news and statistics for the 2018-2019 academic year. The design of the cover features an 'a'ali'i, a native Hawaiian plant, and is used to portray the theme of 'resilience' to represent the school year.

ADOBE ILLUSTRATOR DESIGN

DESIGN DRAFTS

The Great Lawn

One of the alternative directions for the design theme. Focus is on the Great Lawn, the center of the campus.

'A'ali'i

A different illustration style of the a'ali'i. For this style, I used a fuzzy watercolor brush.

'A'ali'i Version 2

A different color scheme for the final design. Went with a darker color background and white text.

PROJECT STATEMENT

Featuring the 'a'ali'i on the cover was meant to represent the college in a positive light. Kapi'olani Community College has gone through many ordeals and changes this year, including changes in staff and throughout the campus itself. The 'a'ali'i is a strong plant that is known to grow in areas with strong, heavy winds. This theme of resilience is a testament to the college in that we are an institution ready to adapt to and endure any challenges we are faced with.
