Branding: Radiant Art Supplies

Project Details

This is a branding project for a fictional brand. Radiant Art Supplies is an art supplies company that sells quality art products, at affordable prices. The deliverables for this project include the packaging, logo, and catalogue.

Process

The process involved creating brand assets for the brand. Once I decided on the company's backstory and target audience, I started sketching designs for a logo. With the logo completed, the next step was to begin creating other brand assets, such as color palettes, photography, illustrations, and more that could be used for the brand. Through trial and error, I finalized brand assets with feedback from the class. The next step was to begin designing the packaging. I wanted to incorporate my colorful tile pattern so i decided to expreiment with ways to apply it to the box. My featured product was a box of liquid watercolor. Next was the catalogue, which featured a two-page layout listing paints and available colors.
