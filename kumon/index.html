Kumon

OBJECTIVE

Re-design an existing logo for a corporation/business.

RESULTS

Kumon is a learning center that tutors children in english, math, and science. The logo re-design for this project focuses on revising Kumon's mission- to encourage students to learn and grow.

ORIGINAL LOGO

The original Kumon logo features what the company calls the thinking face. However, I considered the face to look more sad than thinking when I first saw it. For this reason, my main goal in designing the logo was to portray Kumon in a more positive light.
