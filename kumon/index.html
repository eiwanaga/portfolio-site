Kumon Logo

Project Details

This project is a logo re-design for Kumon. Kumon is a company that tutors children and young adults in math, reading, and science.

Process

The process involved extensive research into the business and its background, including its values, story, and its target audience. Using the research findings, I began a word list to come up with ideas for a logo. Next, I began to sketch possible logo designs for the company. Once I narrowed designs down to 3, I chose one to go with after consulting my professor and started finalizing the design.
